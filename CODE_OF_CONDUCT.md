# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors to be respectful, direct, and constructive.

- assume good intent
- challenge ideas without attacking people
- keep reviews factual and actionable
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and hostile behavior

## Enforcement

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, contact the repository
maintainers privately. Public issue threads are not the right place for conduct
reports.
